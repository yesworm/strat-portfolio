The Missing Middle

Brolly Goods Co. · Ecommerce UX Optimisation · Q1 2024

TL;DR

🚨 Problem: Ad traffic landed but didn't convert.

💡 Insight: Users rarely scrolled, and PDPs were slow.

🎯 Fix: Curated PLPs, faster PDPs, and data-led tweaks.


They had the traffic. But not the traction.

Brolly Goods Co. had just relaunched their ecommerce site. Paid campaigns were working — sessions were strong, but conversions lagged. Product pages weren't being seen. Carts stayed empty. This is what we uncovered, and what we optimised.

🚨 The Problem

Product listing pages (PLPs) weren't converting. Product detail pages (PDPs) were barely being reached. Despite great creative and solid inventory, users weren't making it past the first scroll.

🔍 The Insights

  • 📉 Scroll fatigue: Most mobile users dropped off after the third product row.
  • 🐢 Slow PDPs: Nearly 40% of product pages took over 3s to load.
  • 🎯 Missed conversion cues: Some high-interest products had low add-to-cart rates due to missing trust elements or poor image setups.

🛠️ The Fix

  • Built curated landing pages to better match campaign traffic
  • Optimised PDP load speed and added inline reviews + delivery info
  • Redesigned PLPs to prioritise high-performing products early
  • Tracked scroll depth and product visibility to guide future decisions

📈 The Impact

Product page views increased by 28%, add-to-cart rose by 17%, and mobile conversions improved by 9% over 4 weeks. Cross-sell CTR doubled after PDP adjustments.

🧰 Tools & Methods

  • GA4 for funnel mapping and traffic segmentation
  • Hex dashboards for behavioural analysis and strategy reporting
  • BigQuery + pandas for event and scroll pattern breakdowns

🌱 Takeaway

The launch is just the beginning. Strategic insight post-launch is where real growth lives. Scroll data, PDP friction, and product-level conversion insights helped turn this ecommerce experience from pretty to performant.
